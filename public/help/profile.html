Help on Profile

Your profile contains all your personal information, including your user name (which can be changed) and your email. Viewing and saving your profile serves to validate its contents. So if you had a warning about an email problem or an old profile, that will fix it.

Updating certain fields will cause your profile to be "updated". When your profile is updated, it will appear in the next issue of the Flame News. The fields that cause this are marked with an asterisk in the edit form.

PhotosYou can now add photos (or other assets) to your profile.

These are the standard assets used all over the site, so typically a title, graphic, caption. You can search here for existing assets or you can create a new asset. Assets have ids, and they get attached to your profile by simply listing the ids. You should probably be able to see how to remove one. If you don't have any assets listed, then the system will find one with your name on it, and stick it in here. Specifying your own assets stops that nonsense.

You can add several assets, but 2 or 3 is probably reasonable. How about one of you then, one of you now, and one of you in some embarrassing event that you wish no-one remembers but everyone does.

If you click on "Search assets" or "Create an asset", a new window will pop up with either the search form or the asset creation form. To create an asset, specify the url to the asset (can be local or remote) OR upload a file. Give it a title and optional caption, and click submit. The asset will be created and id number will drop into your asset list on your profile. If you search for an asset, you will get a list of matching items, and a button that says "Send ID" or something like that, and it will drop the selected asset id into your asseet list.
