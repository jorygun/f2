Help on the Opportunity Manager

"Opportunities" are employment opportunities. They are listed under the Opportunties menu, which also shows a count of how many there are. If you click on an Opportunity there, you can view it, and edit if its yours. If you click on the menu title Opportunity, it shows you all open ones, plus a link to create a new one.

Any Flames member can create an opportunity.

Opportunity Data

Title Job title, normally
Location Job location, e.g, "Boston, USA", Edinburgh, "Noo York City!"
Contact The name and email of the contact person. A new contact starts with your name and email, but you can put anything in here.
Link to URL Enter the url to the job description or company site or whatever makes sense.
Expiration Job opportunities expire after 90 days. If the job has been filled you can expire it Now to make it go away.
Description List job info. You can paste from Word into here.
